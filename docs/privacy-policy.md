# AKRIVO Skin Privacy Policy

AKRIVO Skin is built around data minimization and user control.

## Data Collected

- Email address and password hash.
- Name, age range, country or region, skin type, concerns, allergies or sensitivities, current routine, budget level, and sensitivity level.
- Consent records, including photo ownership, AI processing, wellness-only understanding, and guardian consent where applicable.
- Uploaded skin or progress photos.
- AI-generated skin wellness insights, confidence labels, routine suggestions, red flags, reminder settings, progress notes, Duo preferences, and routine completion status.

AKRIVO Skin does not collect phone numbers, SMS identifiers, WhatsApp identifiers, Aadhaar, PAN, government IDs, payment data, or precise location in this MVP.

## Photo Use

Photos are used only to provide skin wellness insights and progress tracking. In the MVP, photos are stored in private local storage outside the public web root. In production, photos should be stored in private object storage or processed temporarily when retention is not needed.

## Retention and Deletion

Users can delete individual progress photo entries, delete analysis history, delete progress timelines, export user data, and delete their account. Photos and related records are retained until deleted by the user or according to a production retention schedule.

## Sharing

Data is not sold. Duo partners can only see routine status, streak, and notes allowed by privacy settings. Face photos are never shared by default.

If an external AI provider is connected, photos and questionnaire context may be processed by that provider under the deployment contract. API keys stay backend-only.

## Children and Teens

AKRIVO Skin asks for age range. For users under the legally required age in their region, parent or guardian consent is required. AKRIVO Skin must not use targeted advertising for children and must not manipulate body image or compare users to unrealistic beauty standards.

## User Rights

Depending on region, users may request access, export, deletion, correction, objection, restriction, or withdrawal of consent.
