## Data Safety Summary

AKRIVO Skin collects account email, profile basics, skin concern selections, uploaded photos, progress notes, AI-generated wellness insights, reminder settings, and Duo sharing preferences. Photos are sensitive user data and must be marked as collected for app functionality.

## AI Disclaimer

AKRIVO Skin provides skincare guidance and wellness insights only. It is not a medical device, does not provide medical diagnosis, and does not replace advice from a qualified dermatologist or doctor.

## Photo Permission Explanation

Photo access is used so users can upload their own skin or progress photos for skin wellness insights and self-tracked progress.

## Account Deletion

Users can delete their account from Settings. Production deployments should also publish a web deletion request path.
